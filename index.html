Southbank Centre
  • Address:Belvedere Rd, London SE1 8XX
  • Get directions with Citymapper
  • Website: southbankcentre.co.uk
  • Open hours: Monday - Sunday: 10am - 11pm
  • Tel: 020 7960 4200
  • Etiquette: No membership required, and you don’t need to buy anything, the wifi is a free open network and hundreds of people come every day to use the space for meetings, lunch dates, work and study groups or solo working. Just be careful not to invade the members bar, or laptop-free zone on the top level, especially if you’re not a member.

Google Campus Cafe
  • Address:4-5 Bonhill Street, London, EC2A 4BX
  • Get directions with Citymapper
  • Website: www.campus.co
  • Open hours: Monday - Friday, 9am-5.30pm
  • Etiquette: Very relaxed. You don’t need to buy anything if you don’t want. The place gets really busy by about 10am so arrive early to ensure you get a decent desk space.

Ace Hotel, Shoreditch
  • Address: 100 Shoreditch High Street, London E1 6JQ
  • Get directions with Citymapper
  • Website: www.acehotel.com/london
  • Open hours: Open 24/7 BUT in the evenings they sometimes have music events, which might be distracting
  • Tel: 020 7613 9800
  • Etiquette: No membership required, and no obligation to buy anything. Anyone can come and use this space for work without any obligation, and there are a fair amount of power sockets around. The wifi is on an open network.

National Art Library (V&A)
  • Address:Victoria & Albert Museum, Cromwell Rd, London SW7 2RL
  • Get directions with Citymapper
  • Website: www.vam.ac.uk
  • Open hours:

    Monday: Closed

    Tuesday: 10am–5:30pm

    Wednesday: 10am–5:30pm

    Thursday: 10am–5:30pm

    Friday: 10am–6:30pm

    Saturday: 10am–5:30pm

    Sunday: Closed

  • Tel:02079422000
  • Etiquette: You need to sign up to become a member, its free for anyone to sign up, you just need to show some current ID with proof of address. You can register inside the library (takes about 5 mins) or you can register in advance here. You need to leave your bags in the cloakroom, which is free only if you’re a member, so try and register first, otherwise cloakroom (in the main entrance hall) is roughly £1 per item. For more info, its worth checking out the conditions of use page

Pancras Square Library
  • Address: 5 Pancras Square London N1C 4AG
  • Get directions with Citymapper
  • Website: www.camden.gov.uk
  • Open hours: open hours
  • Tel: 020 7974 5555
  • Etiquette: Pancras Square Library is a great place for team / group working, meetings, or just working alone. Unlike most libraries, there's no pressure to work in silence, certainly not in the dedicated workspaces at the back, but that's not to say its a noisey environment, its fairly quiet. Wifi is on a free open network, and the space is free for anyone to use.
